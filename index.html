Ping Pong Game!!!!

Instructions

  1. Your paddle is the red on, control it by moving your wrist up and down.
  2. Bounce the ball off your paddle.
  3. Enemy paddle is basically impossible to beat, so stay alive as much as possible.
  4. First one to get 4 points wins.
  5. Have Fun!
